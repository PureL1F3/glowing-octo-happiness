Registration

Creating a StreamHire profile allows employers to find you for the jobs you're interested in, as well as saving you time when filling out job applications.

On StreamHire, when employers post a job they can immediately see anonymous profiles (just resume and availability) of job seekers who match their job based on job interests and search radius. If you receive a job application invitation, that means the employer liked your resume and availability, and would be interested in you applying for their job.

The power to apply for any job is always in your hands. StreamHire never discloses your contact information to any employer until you submit that information in a job application. You can apply to any StreamHire job, wether you find it through StreamHire search or you receive a job application invite from an employer.
